Why This Project?

Due to the pandamic our health system is working under pressure many patients are admitted at a time. If doctors are unable to track medical history then the treatment gets delayed and this is very harmfull for the health of patient.

Solution offered by our project

By storing the all medical history at one place online the Doctors can see your history any time from any place. It helps to get speed to the treatments.

Extra Features of Project?

Using the ML algorithum we are also tring to check the heart health of the User.
